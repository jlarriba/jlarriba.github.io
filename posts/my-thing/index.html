My Thing

01.06.2020 16:29

So, a new blog has born on the 21st century internet. No blogging platform, no Medium, no content aggregation, no social media. Just a good old fashioned web page where a guy rambles against things or supports other things. We’ll see.

Who’s that me that is going to be the star of the thing? Software engineer, systems administrator, mix of both, father, lover. You can find all the interesting crap in my Linkedin profile, here is irrelevant. I am old enough to remember the old internet, before the companies and the market were interested in this no-man-land.

I used to write a blog, many years ago. Back then, blogs were just diaries people used to pour over the internet without worrying about marketing, monetization or even worse followers were a thing. When all that thing appeared, I moved myself away.

No Facebook, no Google+, a couple of Medium posts, a Twitter account that has not published anything for months. No Yotube collection or Instagram account. Not a fan of social media networks, uh? The best chance to get in touch is Github. I have some published projects there and hopefully more to come, trying to give back to the community a tiny piece of what

Anyway, I just wanted a little space on this (previously) decentralized network to talk a little about everything. Do not expect to much coherence, as people change when they learn: it was called growing. Expect a lot of topics on my favourite subject: “ramblings of an old man”.

See you in space, space cowboy!
